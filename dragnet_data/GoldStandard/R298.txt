Ghesquière's vision as seen by Dazed, as we delve through archive magazine editorial

Earlier today, we showcased the Dazed fashion team's pick of Nicolas Ghesquière's runway looks for Balenciaga, celebrating their favourite top-to-toe outfits as proposed by the designer. Now we delve into the Dazed archive to see Ghesquière's clothes as interpreted by the magazine's stylists over the years, from Katie England and Cathy Edwards to current senior contributing fashion editor Katie Shillingford and fashion director Karen Langley. This is an anthology of the avantgarde.
