NFL Fantasy LIVE podcast (10/30/12)

NFL Fantasy Live – October 30, 2012 Hour 1

Check out this week’s edition of the “NFL Fantasy Live” podcast as Jason Smith, Michael Fabiano and Elliot Harrison tackle all your fantasy questions heading into Week 9. The crew re-drafts the first round at the midway point of 2012. Also, Jerry Jones has all the answers for the Cowboys’ struggles. That and more in the first hour.

NFL Fantasy Live – October 30, 2012 Hour 2

In the final hour of the “NFL Fantasy Live” podcast, The Fantasy Football Cantina gets some overtime as the guys have some major breaking news on the “Star Wars” front. Also, Jason, Fabs and Elliot try to help you get some answers on the toughest 50-50 fantasy plays going into Week 9. It’s organized chaos at its finest.
