Ex-Anglo Irish Executives Charged

   

BY EAMON QUINN

DUBLIN—Authorities in Ireland arrested and indicted two former executives of Anglo Irish Bank Corp. for allegedly encouraging a huge share-support plan, marking the first charges to be brought in an investigation of the lender that was central to the country's banking crisis.
