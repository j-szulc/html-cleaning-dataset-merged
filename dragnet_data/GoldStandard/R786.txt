Everybody's a Critic

The classroom hasn't escaped the food writing bug, as 9-year old Liam Waldman recounts his recent experience at Michael Voltaggio's ink restaurant in Los Angeles: "I carefully chose one meal after another the courses were very tiny and very unexpected the food came lightning fast apr. 2 min. and very pleasently.[sic]" Waldman continues with "most beautiful dish came out snap peas it might sound pretty ordinary but it was very out of the ordinry. It was also very equisite and a looottt of attention to detail." A spell-checker might be the only steps left for this budding critic. [LA Weekly]
