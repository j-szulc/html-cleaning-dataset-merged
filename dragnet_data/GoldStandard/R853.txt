
VINCI Curriculum Named as CES Innovations 2013 Design and Engineering Awards Honoree
By Eliane Fiolet	on 11/12/2012 15:23 PST	

VINCI Curriculum is the software exclusively running in the VINCI Tab II and the VINCI Tab II M (photo above) , two tablets specifically designed for kids. VINCI curriculum is tailored to help children build learning capabilities. It features 43 monthly themes and topics across 3 levels of learning: The Curious; The Confident; and The Capable.

We all know that toddler are in love with user friendly touch screens and tablets, although we did not try it we can imagine that the VINCI system is certainly a great product to infuse the love of learning in kids. And now it got an award to prove it.
