Fractured Soul - Three new videos on Nintendo eShop - November 1, 2012

2012-11-01

Three new videos are now available in the Nintendo eShop to give players a special look at Fractured Soul. Find out what makes the game so unique, discover some of the thoughts behind development and learn more about speed-running tricks.

Starting on 11/1/2012, and includes the following videos:

Introduction

What is Fractured Soul? Find out what it’s all about and why it’s so unique.

Rhythm & Flow

Get into the flow of Fractured Soul. Discover how the game was developed with a certain gameplay rhythm in mind.

Speedrunning

Climb the leaderboard in no time. Learn these speed running tricks to get ahead of the rest.
