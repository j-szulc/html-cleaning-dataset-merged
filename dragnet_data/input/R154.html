Spinlet - Share Your Music With Friends

Published on December 3, 2009 in Internet/E-Mail Tools, Multimedia Tools and Personal Tools. Tags: facebook, media, music, share, spinlet, twitter.

Twitter and Facebook are very popular these days. There are a few applications for connecting and sharing information in social networks.

If you like to share your favorite songs with your friends, Spinlet is a great application for you.

All you have to do is setting your Facebook or Twitter username and password. The application will automatically share your favorite songs.

Spinlet automatically finding your saved songs on your phone. You don’t have to browse them manually. You can also create new playlists with Spinlet.

This application is Freeware and I hope it will remain free.

You can find more information on their Website
