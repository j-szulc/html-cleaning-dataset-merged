A Beautiful Giveaway with Feral Watches

We’re so excited to bring you a giveaway from this lovely online timepiece store. Feral Watches creates beautiful watches inspired by Hawaii’s northern shore. They have these wonderful six styles now (1, 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6), and they’re looking to make even more available. But they need your help! Check out their Kickstarter to see how you can get your hands on a new design before anyone else.
Today we’re giving one lucky winner 2 watches of their choosing. To enter, just like them on Facebook, then visit their store and post your favorite in the comments. Giveaway will be open until Nov. 20, and the winner will be chosen shortly after. Good luck! xoxo. Elsie + Emma
