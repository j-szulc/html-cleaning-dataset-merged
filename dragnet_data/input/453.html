BY LAURA MECKLER

SAN FRANCISCO—The presidential candidates jumped back into campaign mode Monday after a weekend pause from political punch and counterpunch amid grieving for the dozen people killed in Aurora, Colo.

Republican Mitt Romney resumed his criticism of President Barack Obama for his July 13 comments that Americans succeed through their collective efforts. And for the first time, Mr. Obama shot back, accusing Mr. Romney of distorting his words.
