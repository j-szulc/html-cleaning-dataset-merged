UNSC Weapons Revealed In New Halo 4 Trailer

Published on 09/08/2012 by Nicholas Griffin in Gaming, Videos
  • Tweet
  • Pin It

You may remember that the Spartan Laser was leaked on video yesterday, well 343 Industries have just 1 upped that with a look at all of the UNSC weapons available in Halo 4′s multiplayer.

A lot of the weapons within the trailer you are likely to have seen before, but there are a few new ones, check out the trailer above for a look at all of them.

Halo 4 will be released on November 26th for Xbox 360.
