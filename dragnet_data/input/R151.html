Advanced Battery Saver With 2 Licences

Published on March 17, 2010 in Personal Tools and System Tools. Tags: battery saver, Melon Mobile, Nokia, solution.

Advanced Battery Saver is one of the best solution to save your battery live. It’s coming with battery optimization models to extend your battery life.

The developer claim that it’s increasing your battery life up to %30. That sounds great! You may be curious about how this application saves battery. It closing some unnecessary applications like (Bluetooth, Sounds and Vibrations, Unnecessary service applications, Lights etc…)

It have 3 battery optimization modes; Basic saver, Saver off and Max Saver. You can also create a new mode in Options > Create New section.

- In Basic Saver Mode; Application is shutting down some “Battery Drainer” applications.
- In Max Saver Mode; Network Features, Bluetooth and Vibration is disabling automatically. That only allows you to use phone function. (That may be useful when you don’t have a charger on public places)

If you select saver off mode, It turns off power saving.

I also liked “When battery is” feature. It’s automatically enables saver when your battery is dropped under your defined level.
