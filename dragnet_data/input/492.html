Lehman, MF Global Dominate Distressed Debt Trading

BY JACQUELINE PALANK

Distressed investors bought and sold $2.34 billion worth of bankruptcy claims last month, nearly all of which is tied to Lehman Brothers Holdings Inc. and MF Global Inc.

About $2.2 billion worth of Lehman and MF Global claims traded hands in June, according to data released Tuesday by claims trading market operator SecondMarket Inc., meaning that the wind-downs of those two financial firms accounted for 94% of the debt that traded last month.
